About Us
We are some students from the IB May 2021 batch. Having gone through 2 years of the diploma program, working on several essays, and giving exams, we had a collection of resources which we thought might be useful to you. This website contains some of our essays with the grades they got and some resources in the form of student notes, websites, and other online resources that we used. We hope you find these resources helpful. Get in touch with us if you need any additional help/guidance. Good luck with IB!
